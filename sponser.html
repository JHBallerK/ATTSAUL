SPONSORS AND PARTNERS



Funder/Sponsor’s input

The participation from stakeholder/sponsor can take various forms could include the following:

  • Direct financial contribution towards the specific project budget (most desired)
  • Provide assistance (e.g. resource, equipment, material, instruments etc.)
  • Finance a section of the specific total item budget

Sponsorship Agreement

What the organisation and its programs have to offer for such assistance is public recognition, exposure, credits and profile image building.

The number of assistance prospects is varied, but would aim at providing “Value for money” sponsor recognition opportunities or, in business terms, return on investments opportunities.


Financial Accountability

An accountant is appointed to audit and check on the finances of the organisation. The treasure shall keep proper records of all finances and assets, the chairperson and two other members of the organisation will sign the withdrawal or cheques.Book keeping functions, petty cash, budgeting, financial Reporting

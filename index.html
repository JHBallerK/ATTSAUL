ATTSAUL

ARTS AND CULTURE FORUM


ATTSAUL ARTS AND CULTURE FORUM was established in Attridgeville/Saulsville (Phelindaba) township of Tswhane to develop and create opportunities for our community especially youth in arts, culture and heritage sector. Registered in 2015, the forum aims to develop and assist in job creation for the artists. South Africa has high levels of unemployment and drugs related problems, this is particularly true for the youth of our country. It is in the rural and township areas of this country that have been particularly hit by the scourge. ATTSAUL ARTS AND CULTURE FOURM will implement programmes, which we aim to start as soon we get the resources in place. Workshops will be conducted in different schools and community at large to highlight the need for training in arts, culture and heritage sector. The training will be accredited; they will also take place in the afternoon (after school) and Saturdays in order to accommodate daily school goers. We would also like to RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) our artist since most of them can assist in training if assessed against their skill and certificated.



Saulsville Arena is based in Atteridgeville, allocated by Tshwane Metro Municipality to arts, culture and heritage practitioners and organisations to implement development programmes in our community ,this venue accommodates up to 3500 sitting and 2000 standing audience We hope to utilise this infrastructure for the development and sustainability of our artists, we also working towards partnership with business, local, provincial, national and international governments to be part of this initiative Primary schools, Institutions of higher learning, institutions for people with Disabilities and all arts, culture and heritage groups, organisations and practitioners will be part of these developments programmes.



Most importantly, this will be the beginning of the steps to establish the Arena as Arts and culture business hub ,we aim to initiate events to encourage participation by creating on-going platforms to allow artists their first real opportunity to start business in this facility which will attract tourism and community as customers. With these initiatives we hope to contribute towards assisting the government to achieve its NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT PLAN (NDP) . The beneficiaries of these initiatives will be Attridgeville, Saulsville and Lotus gardens arts and culture organisations, practitioners and the community at large, we would like to form partnership with other Forums other sectors to join hands with us in partnership to make a success of this empowerment dream With all indicated above the forum aims to reach its goal â€œTo empower the community and its youth to gain meaningful entry into arts and culture industry locally, regionally, nationally and internally"

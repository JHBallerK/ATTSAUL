BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The structure of the forum could be compared to the structure of an association.


Different groups that are affiliated to the forum are not necessarily incubated but are assisted to the forum in terms of development, promotion and availability of resources.


They are all independent groups running their own affairs. We identify areas that needs support a development which our different/ stand-alone commissions will then facilitate professionals to assist in the development.


ATTSAUL implements different programs which organisations responds too according to their needs, once this is done whatever quality products produced during this process will then be outsourced. Most importantly initiatives will be taken to establish a business hub at the infrastructure provided by the Tshwane Municipality; Saulsville Arena to sustain productivity to supply to different clients to including your organisation.


With all indicated above, the forum aims to achieve its goals to empower Arts and Cultural Organisation, Practitioner and Community at large in the industry.
